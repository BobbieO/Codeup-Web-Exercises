10 FAQs About National Parks in Arizona

Does Montezuma Castle park have bathrooms?
Nope, plan ahead.
Can we climb up and go inside?
No. It's crumbly, and liability insurance is a thing.
Can I keep a piece of the Petrified Forest for my very own?
Only if you buy it in the gift shop. Otherwise, there's a Big Fine for stealing relics.
Can I pet the squirrels and chipmunks in Arizona National Parks?
I wouldn't. They can carry bubonic plague.
How do you pronounce Saguaro?
"Sa-WAH-ro"
Will the Sunset Crater Volcano erupt while I'm there?
Probably not.
Can I climb to the top?
No, others ruined the trail before you so now no one can climb it.
Can I bring my dog to Casa Grande Ruins?
Yes, but it's hot, so monitor your dog. Also, he/she should be well-trained.
Can I off-road in Canyon de Chelly?
Absolutely not.
Will my cell phone work in the parks?
Possibly. But you should look at the scenery instead.
